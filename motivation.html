Motivation Theory:

Motivation is the reason for peoples actions, willingness and goals. There are 4 types of motivation. Extrinsic motivation, which is a motivation that we find outside ourselves, Intrinsic motivation is the motivation that comes from within and is considered a reason of being (ikigai), Introjected motivation is the same as intrinsic motivation but leaves the person with guilt if its not done. and identified motivation where a person knows that something needs to be done but has yet decided to do anything about it.

Ted Talk: Dan Pink

- According to him, what is motivation?
Motivation is based around 2 facts. Intrinsic motivation and extrinsic motivation. Back in the day when companies wanted to see growth, they often stimulated their employees with extrinsic motivators. They gave you a task to do and reward you with money or some external way of appreciation. According to what Daniel explains those ways of motivation are actually holding back most of the people of their own growth and creativity and let business stagnate.
- What determines someone’s motivation?
According to science Daniel explains 3 element that are stimulating people intrinsicly and shows a tremendous increase in creativity and growth in business.
Autonomy: the urge to direct our own lives
Mastery: the desire to get better at something that matters
Purpose: the earning to do in the service of something bigger then ourselves
- How can you influence and manage your own motivation?
Daniel explained that this new way of motivating people instrinsicly is implemented in a lot of big tech companies and the results are remarkable. Those companies gave their employees more autonomy throughout the year. They wanted them to spend more time of their work on their own projects, this causes people to feel more autonomous in various ways and new innovative ideas were born.
